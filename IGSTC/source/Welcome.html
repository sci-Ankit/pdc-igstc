Accomodation

Taramani Guest House (TGH) is an institute-provided boarding and lodging facility situated inside the campus for delegates attending academic events. TGH usually runs at capacity due to the various activities happening each day, so delegates should take note to book a room in advance. Please contact us to check availability and make a booking.

We have arranged for affordable accomodation at Hotel Ginger, which is situated within the IITM Research Park. You can book your rooms through this link.
