Accomodation

Taramani Guest House (TGH) is an institute-provided boarding and lodging facility situated inside the campus for delegates attending academic events. TGH usually runs at capacity due to the various activities happening each day, so delegates should take note to book a room in advance.

IIT Madras have multiple hostel, they are available for workshop attendees on request at an affordable price. You can know more about them here.

We have arranged for affordable accomodation at Hotel Ginger, which is situated within the IITM Research Park. You can know more about them here.
